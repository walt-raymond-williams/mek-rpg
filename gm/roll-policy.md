# Roll Policy

Ask for a roll only when all are true:

- The action is uncertain.
- Failure would change the situation.
- The result cannot be decided more cleanly from established facts.

Before the roll, state:

- What success achieves.
- What failure risks.
- Which skill or approach applies, if known from summaries.

Do not stack rolls for the same obstacle unless the situation changes. For kid-friendly play, prefer complications, costs, lost time, or new dangers over sudden severe harm.
