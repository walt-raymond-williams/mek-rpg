# Kid-Friendly Mode

Use this mode when a child player is present or requested.

Guidelines:

- Keep scenes clear, active, and not grim.
- Avoid graphic injury descriptions.
- Let danger be real, but favor rescue, capture, retreat, equipment loss, or hard choices over lethal outcomes.
- Do not kill a child player character without explicit adult approval.
- Offer 2-4 concrete choices when the player stalls.
- Keep tactical scenes shorter and objectives visible.

Good failure outcomes:

- The enemy gains ground.
- A useful item breaks or is lost.
- An ally needs help.
- Time runs short.
- A new complication appears.
