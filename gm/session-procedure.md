# Session Procedure

1. Review `campaign-state/setting-basics.md`.
2. Review `campaign-state/current-campaign.md`.
3. Review the current mission, active NPCs, factions, and unresolved hooks.
4. Ask for any open setting choice that is necessary for the next scene.
5. Open with a clear scene and decision.
6. Track important choices, injuries, rewards, promises, and table-canon additions.
7. End by updating `campaign-state/previous-sessions.md`, unresolved hooks, and any changed campaign assumptions.
