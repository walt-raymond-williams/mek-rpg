# Tactical Encounter Template

Use `gm/tactical-encounter-handoff-checklist.md` before filling this template when the encounter comes from an active campaign save.

## Encounter Name
TBD

## Trigger
What event moves the session from RPG mode to tactical combat.

## Battlefield
Terrain, visibility, weather, deployment zones, and notable hazards.

## Player Forces
Units, pilots, starting positions, damage, ammunition, and objectives.

## RPG Skill Bridge
Relevant A Time of War Piloting, Driving, Gunnery, Artillery, Sensor Operations, Tactics, Leadership, Technician, or Navigation notes. Record converted tactical ratings and assumptions from `rules/vehicles-and-mechs/converting-to-classic-battletech.md`.

## Opposing Forces
Units, pilots, starting positions, morale, and objectives.

## Victory Conditions
Primary and secondary conditions for each side.

## Withdrawal Conditions
When either side can or should leave the field.

## Campaign Consequences
Salvage, repairs, injuries, reputation, contract changes, and future hooks.

## Return-To-Campaign Updates
List the exact campaign files that must change after tactical resolution, especially `session-log.md`, `current-state.md`, `missions.md`, `assets.md`, `pcs.md`, `npcs.md`, `factions.md`, `relationships.md`, `hooks.md`, and `pending-mekhq-actions.md` for MekHQ-linked campaigns.
