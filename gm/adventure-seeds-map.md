# Adventure Seeds Map

## Purpose

Use this routing aid when the GM wants to inspect the source adventure seed section, build an original mission from a seed concept, or record a campaign hook without reproducing published seed text.

## When to Use

- The GM needs a quick scenario prompt and wants to inspect the owned source pages.
- A campaign hook should be turned into a mission template.
- The table wants an adventure premise that connects to a faction, location, patron, mystery, combat threat, or moral complication.

## Do Not Use For

- Copying, paraphrasing in detail, or publishing the source adventure seed entries.
- Treating seed prompts as canon events unless the active campaign adopts them.
- Replacing mission preparation or active campaign save updates.

## Routing Procedure

1. Inspect the cited source pages privately if the GM wants one of the published seed prompts.
2. Convert the chosen idea into an original mission record using `gm/mission-template.md`.
3. Define:
   - employer or inciting pressure
   - real stakes
   - objective
   - opposition
   - first complication
   - reward or consequence
   - state files to update afterward
4. If the seed depends on faction, rank, title, or world canon, mark those facts as lookup-needed until verified.
5. Record the finished hook in the active campaign save, not in this routing file.

## Original Scaffold

Use this table-created scaffold when you do not need a source seed:

- Someone wants the PCs to move, protect, find, expose, repair, steal, escort, negotiate, or survive something.
- A local authority, rival, employer, dependent, or hidden actor benefits if the PCs fail.
- The obvious objective is incomplete or misleading.
- One faction consequence and one personal consequence will persist after the mission.
