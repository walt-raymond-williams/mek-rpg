# Switch to Classic BattleTech

Stop narrating RPG combat and generate a Classic BattleTech, MegaMek, or MekHQ encounter setup when tactical detail matters more than scene-level resolution.

Switch when one or more are true:

- BattleMechs, combat vehicles, aerospace units, or full squads are central to the fight.
- Facing, hex movement, terrain, heat, armor locations, ammunition, or weapon range brackets matter.
- The players care about exact positioning or tactical survival.
- Multiple units on each side need initiative and turn-by-turn handling.
- The outcome should affect salvage, repairs, force strength, or campaign logistics.

Encounter setup should include:

- Map environment and terrain assumptions.
- Forces on each side.
- Pilot quality or RPG-derived pilot/gunnery notes.
- Objectives and withdrawal conditions.
- Special rules or scenario constraints.
- What campaign state should be updated after the fight.

Stay in RPG mode when the scene is a brief chase, intimidation, sabotage, escape, or single decisive roll where tactical positioning is not important.
