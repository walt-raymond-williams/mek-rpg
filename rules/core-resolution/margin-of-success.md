# Margin of Success
## Purpose
What this rule is for.
## When to Use
Use this rule when the degree of success or failure matters.
## Do Not Use For
Cases where another subsystem should be used.
## Basic Procedure
1. Resolve the underlying check.
2. Determine how far the result is from the target or opposing result.
3. Apply any verified effects tied to the degree of result.
## Practical GM Guidance
Use margin to scale speed, quality, information gained, or collateral consequences.
## Common Edge Cases
- Edge case: A binary pass/fail result may be enough.
- Edge case: The source may define specific margin thresholds that need review.
