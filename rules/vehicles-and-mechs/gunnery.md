# Gunnery
## Purpose
What this rule is for.
## When to Use
Use this rule when a character fires vehicle or BattleMech weapons outside or adjacent to tactical combat.
## Do Not Use For
Cases where another subsystem should be used.
## Basic Procedure
1. Identify weapon platform, target, range, and scene scale.
2. Decide whether RPG resolution or tactical combat is appropriate.
3. Apply verified gunnery procedure or switch systems.
## Practical GM Guidance
If hit location, heat, armor, or exact range matters, switch to Classic BattleTech.
## Common Edge Cases
- Edge case: Personal weapons use personal combat rules.
- Edge case: Aerospace or artillery may need special handling.
