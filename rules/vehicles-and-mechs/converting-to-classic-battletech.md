## Purpose
What this rule is for.

Use this rule when RPG characters need tactical BattleTech pilot values or scenario setup.

1. Identify the RPG character skills and vehicle role.
2. Apply verified conversion procedure or table-approved placeholder.
3. Create tactical notes for MegaMek, MekHQ, or tabletop play.
## Practical GM Guidance
Do not invent exact conversion math. If unverified, state that the conversion needs source review.

- Edge case: Elite RPG characters may not map cleanly to tactical ratings.
