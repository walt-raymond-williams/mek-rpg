# Contacts
## Purpose
What this rule is for.
## When to Use
Use this rule when NPC relationships provide help, information, pressure, or complications.
## Do Not Use For
Cases where another subsystem should be used.
## Basic Procedure
1. Identify the contact, relationship, and request.
2. Apply verified contact or social procedure if available.
3. Record favors, debts, changed attitudes, or new hooks.
## Practical GM Guidance
Give contacts names, limits, and motives so they are playable.
## Common Edge Cases
- Edge case: A contact may become an NPC patron or antagonist.
- Edge case: Mechanical contact benefits need source review.
