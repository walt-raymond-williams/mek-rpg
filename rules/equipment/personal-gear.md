# Personal Gear
## Purpose
What this rule is for.
## When to Use
Use this rule when tools, kits, sensors, comms, or survival equipment affect a scene.
## Do Not Use For
Cases where another subsystem should be used.
## Basic Procedure
1. Identify the gear and intended use.
2. Decide whether it grants capability, reduces risk, or changes fictional positioning.
3. Apply verified rules or mark the detail for source review.
## Practical GM Guidance
Reward preparation by reducing rolls, opening options, or softening consequences.
## Common Edge Cases
- Edge case: Gear may require training or power supply.
- Edge case: Availability and legality may matter more than mechanics.
