# Ranged Attacks
## Purpose
What this rule is for.
## When to Use
Use this rule when a character attacks with a ranged personal weapon.
## Do Not Use For
Cases where another subsystem should be used.
## Basic Procedure
1. Identify weapon, target, range, cover, and circumstances.
2. Apply verified attack procedure and modifiers.
3. Resolve hit, damage, and follow-up consequences.
## Practical GM Guidance
Call out cover, movement, ammo concerns, and bystanders before the roll.
## Common Edge Cases
- Edge case: Vehicle or BattleMech weapons may need another subsystem.
- Edge case: Autofire or special weapon rules need source review.
