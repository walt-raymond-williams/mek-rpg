# Term Glossary

This glossary is a placeholder until source review.

- AToW: A Time of War.
- CBT: Classic BattleTech.
- GM: Game Master.
- MegaMek: Digital tool for resolving Classic BattleTech tactical combat.
- MekHQ: Campaign and force-management tool often used with MegaMek.
- Task check: A roll or procedure used to resolve an uncertain action.
- Opposed check: A check where another character or force actively resists.
- Margin of success: The degree by which a check succeeds.
