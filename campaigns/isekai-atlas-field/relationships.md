# Relationships

## Walter and His Son

- Relationship: Parent and child.
- Status: Together at campaign start.
- Trust: Strong family bond, confirmed by premise.
- Immediate pressure: Walter is responsible for keeping his son safe in an unknown world.
- Table guidance: Offer concrete choices and avoid severe harm to the child character without explicit adult approval.

## Walter, Sharp, and Lt. Mara Voss

- Status: First contact under emergency conditions.
- Trust: Mara is vulnerable and warned them not to let "them" take the Atlas.
- Leverage: Walter and Sharp have her ID card and access to the cockpit survival kit.
- Open questions: Who is "them," whether Mara will trust Walter and Sharp, and whether using her Atlas authorization will create consequences.
