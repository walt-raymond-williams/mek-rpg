# Safety And Tone

Use this file to remember player preferences and child/co-player constraints.

## Table Tone

- Tone: Father/son adventure, mystery, discovery, survival, and cinematic BattleTech wonder unless the user changes this.
- Violence detail level: Keep harm non-graphic by default.
- Kid-friendly mode: On by default until the user says otherwise.

## Child Or Co-Player Guidance

- Do not kill a child player character without explicit adult approval.
- Favor rescue, retreat, equipment loss, time pressure, capture, misunderstanding, or hard choices over graphic harm.
- Keep objectives visible and offer 2-4 concrete choices when a younger player stalls.
- Let the Atlas feel impressive and mysterious before making it a lethal rules problem.
