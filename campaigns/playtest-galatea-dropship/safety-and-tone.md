# Safety And Tone

Status: playtest-only; not table canon.

## Table Tone

- Tone: active, clear, lower-grimness adventure suitable for playing with the user's son.
- Violence detail level: avoid graphic harm.
- Kid-friendly mode: yes when the son is present.

## Child Or Co-Player Guidance

- Do not kill a child player character without explicit adult approval.
- Keep objectives visible and concrete.
- Offer 2-4 choices when Sharpe's player stalls.
- Favor discovery, rescue, retreat, equipment complications, time pressure, or hard choices over lethal consequences.

## Player Preferences

- Sharpe worked well as a technical co-protagonist with meaningful inspection rolls.
