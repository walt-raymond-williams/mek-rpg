## Known Gaps Before First Playtest

- Character creation and full PC sheets are not summarized enough for full campaign setup. Use placeholders for the first playtest unless the user supplies characters.
- Long-term campaign injury, recovery consequences, mission readiness, and downtime remain placeholder-level.
- Exact table-heavy values for weapons, armor, gear, modifiers, and some combat details remain source-reference lookups rather than committed summaries.
