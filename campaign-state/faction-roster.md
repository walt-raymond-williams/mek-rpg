## Issue #12 Galatea DropShip Playtest Organizations

Status: Confirmed by user as playtest only; not table canon.

### Rook Interstellar Recovery

- Table role: repossession or recovery organization pressuring DropShip sales on Galatea.
- Current attitude toward PCs: unknown; inferred hostile if the PCs interfere with `SABLE-7` or `The Second Dawn`.
- Known local assets: shell-company transfers, dormant remote command locks, possible port or beacon monitoring, market pressure.
- Important NPCs: no confirmed direct representative; possible connections to Mara Voss, Jax Rill, or unknown dock actors are unconfirmed.
- Open canon questions: legal status, actual faction backing, local authority ties, technical reach, and whether Rook is a one-off playtest invention or future faction seed.

### The Second Dawn Cooperative

- Table role: DropShip ownership cooperative offering the PCs controlling stake and first lift rights.
- Current attitude toward PCs: cautious potential partners.
- Known local assets: Union-class DropShip `The Second Dawn`, crew, captain's badge access to Dock C-19.
- Important NPCs: Captain Selene Arano, Mate Della Kho, Tavi Renn, Brant Kole, Doctor Ilyan Saye.
- Open canon questions: legal ownership, debts/liens, crew loyalties, operating permits, maintenance state, and whether Rook can force default.

### Galatea DropShip Brokerage Market

- Table role: public ship market and pressure point for mercenary transport deals.
- Current attitude toward PCs: transactional; may become wary after Walter walked away from Mara's deposit request.
- Known local assets: broker kiosks, listings, public sales pressure, inspection/certification process.
- Important NPCs: Mara Voss, Jax Rill.
- Open canon questions: official market rules, local enforcement, inspection authority, title-record procedure, and current 3025 transport pricing.
